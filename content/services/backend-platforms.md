Privorum designs and builds backend platforms for products that need to hold up beyond an MVP — reliable APIs, clean service boundaries, and infrastructure that an on-call engineer can actually operate.

We help answer practical questions such as:

- Where should the service boundaries sit, and which ones are premature?
- What does the data model need to look like once the product is a year older?
- How do we make deploys boring and rollbacks fast?
- Which parts of the stack need SLOs, and which don't?
- How do we keep the platform maintainable as the team grows?

## Typical engagement areas

- API design, service decomposition, and platform architecture
- data modelling, migrations, and storage strategy
- CI/CD, deployment pipelines, and infrastructure-as-code
- observability, SLOs, alerting, and incident response
- performance, reliability, and cost tuning

## Technology depth

Golang, Java, Node.js, TypeScript, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, PostgreSQL, Redis, AWS, and related production infrastructure.

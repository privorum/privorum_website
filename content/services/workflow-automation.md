Privorum automates internal workflows, operational processes, and cross-system coordination for teams that are tired of glue code that breaks quietly.

We help answer practical questions such as:

- Which manual steps are actually worth automating, and which are worth keeping explicit?
- How do we connect the services and tools we already use without building a fragile middle layer?
- Where do we need a durable job runner, and where does a simple script suffice?
- How do we make this observable when something fails at 3am?

## Typical engagement areas

- process automation across internal tooling and SaaS
- event-driven services, queues, and job orchestration
- integrations between CRMs, data warehouses, and internal systems
- human-in-the-loop approval flows and audit trails
- operational dashboards, alerting, and runbooks

Automation is only useful when it reduces overhead without adding silent failure modes. We design for both.

Privorum helps companies design and implement permissioned blockchain systems when trust, control, privacy, and operational structure matter more than fully open networks.

We focus on enterprise-grade systems that require clear governance, known participants, strong auditability, and integration with existing business workflows.

## Where permissioned systems make sense

- regulated environments
- multi-party data sharing
- enterprise process coordination
- private transaction flows
- systems that require policy and participant control

## Platforms and architectures

Privorum has worked with and advised around architectures involving:

- Hyperledger
- Corda
- Quorum
- Smilo
- Ethereum-based permissioned models

## What we help with

- architecture and protocol selection
- consensus and privacy tradeoffs
- smart contract and backend integration
- private transaction models
- infrastructure and node operations
- system review and implementation planning

Permissioned blockchain projects succeed when architecture, governance, privacy, and operations are designed deliberately from the start.

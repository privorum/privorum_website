Privorum is a technical consultancy focused on AI systems, backend platforms, workflow automation, and distributed software.

We work with companies that need more than generic implementation support. Our role is to help shape architecture, build reliable services, connect systems, and make technical decisions that hold up in production.

## What we do

Privorum supports products and platforms across:

- AI workflows and agent systems
- workflow automation across internal tooling and operations
- backend APIs and platform engineering
- data pipelines and analytics systems
- cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, and observability
- blockchain and distributed systems when they are strategically relevant

## Technical depth

Our engineering experience spans:

- **Languages** — Golang, Java, Node.js, TypeScript, Solidity
- **Frontend** — React, React Native
- **Infrastructure** — Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, AWS
- **Data** — PostgreSQL, ArangoDB, Redis

That breadth matters because most serious systems are not isolated. They involve product constraints, infrastructure tradeoffs, data flows, integrations, and operational reliability.

## Where blockchain fits

Blockchain remains an important part of Privorum's background, especially in protocol work, marketplaces, trading systems, wallets, analytics platforms, and smart-contract-enabled products.

But the company is not positioned as "blockchain only." The stronger value is the ability to build and operate complex technical systems, including blockchain-native ones when they are the right fit.

## How we work

Privorum works hands-on with founders, operators, and technical teams that need clarity, delivery discipline, and senior engineering judgment.

The goal is not to add noise. The goal is to help teams ship systems that are reliable, maintainable, and commercially useful.
